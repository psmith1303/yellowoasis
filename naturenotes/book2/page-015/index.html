1.3.59
Abberton Resavoir

[Pasted in: Watercolour painting 16cm x 7cm Head of Red Breasted Merganser From specimen sot at Peldon during January 1959. Actual size, correct measurements. ♀ Signed AKW 2.2.59]

Upon arrival at the Layer causeway where I was watching G.C.Grebes, I met up with Joe Firmin who informed me that the eggs of the Black letter hairstreak he has were on the point of hatching and he was out to get some blackthorn shoots for foodstuff for them. Over the causeway were quite a few Pochard, Tufted Duck & a Heron as well as the usual Coots.

By motorcycle, I went round to the other causeway, observing the Rooks building quite fast. In the fields were small flocks of Lapwing with C.Gulls. More Tufted & Pochard were here as well as a few Teal & more G.C.Grebe. One Cormorant wheeled in a landed near a very nice party of Smew, beautiful males with females, & immature birds, there were 15+ approx; A few Mallard were quite near and Moorhens. A.K.W.
