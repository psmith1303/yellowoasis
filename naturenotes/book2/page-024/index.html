A plant R.L. produced on arrival back at my home for identification was a plant of Sea Rocket, he had collected from Lee-over-Sands previous Sunday.

19.4.59 : A quick visit to Friday wood Farm area where I heard my first ‘59 Cuckoo. Collected water weed from Roman River & also caught a small minnow. Heard G. Woodpecker & also Chiffchaffs. A close look on some Sloe bushses produced two yellowish small looper caterpillars I secured one & dropped the other.

21.4.59 : Going to J. Firm Snr house via Elinore Road. I saw my first Swallow of ‘59 during the evening approx: 7-15. After this J. Firmin, Bill Simpson & myself made an expedition for Sloe carpets moths in the spring lane area with Tilley lamp. Bill left at 8.45 as he left for Germany on the following Friday after spending this day with J.F. We had no success with the Sloe Carpets but the only two specimens caught were Early thom, & This was the first expedition of the “New Auralian Society”.
