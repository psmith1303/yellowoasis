26.2.61

Mangles Wood Berechurch With B.F. & G.E. & son Godfrey to see if our mammal traps had caught anything. B.F. set them. One in the chestnut grove & one by the river bank in hope of catching a vole. Neither had been spring & no catch.

We roamed around the wood & through it to the river, here we saw another Kingfisher as we did last week. Green Woodpeckers were heard & seen & we heard the drumming of the G.S.Woodpecker. The L.S.Woodpecker’s nest in the hedge-row was still untouched & we had a quick look round.

Upon coming back to the wood we went to our bikes to get our coffee & sheets & sat under some holly trees to keep watch on a squirrel drey - nothing showed up & we saw none at all. I saw a Nuthatch, & this flew toward another which was calling out. These birds seemed very much in evidence when we first got into the wood calling out quite loud.
