2.4.61
Abberton Resavoir

With B.F. & G.E. & his cousin in a car to have a quick look at the resavoir. Not much to be seen at the first Layer causeway except. Tufted Duck & Widgeon & Coot. A short chat with Terry Dooley & we went round to the other causeway. Not such a lot here but we were pleased to see our first in Essex on passage Wheatear a ♂ bird of which I had good views.

Rather nice were the Reed Buntings along the water edge. Not all that common over here was a Redshank, which flew over the water to the reed beds. More Tufted Ducks in larger flocks. & a Heron flew up & settled again quickly. Great Crested Grebes were giving courtship display - neck to neck, & in good numbers. The weather was cold & windy with rain now & then which made the water choppy. We didn’t stop long & left at about 12’ oclock.
