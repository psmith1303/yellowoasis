Tucked in: 4 black and white photographs, 11cm x 7.5cm.

  1. [June 4th?] Showing 5 kestrel chicks in the nest.

  2. [June 11th?] Showing 4 kestrel chicks in the nest.

  3. As above [duplicate print?]

  4. [June 25th?] Showing immature kestrel being ringed. Two hands shown in the photograph, one wearing a leather gauntlet, the other a signet ring on the ring finger [George Evans?].
