…they flew over, calling quite loud, as did a small party of Grey Plover in summer plumage. Two Shoveller duck flew onto the island & more Shelduck.

We then made our way back along the sea wall watching Oyster Catchers flying off. Many Swallows & Swifts soaring around, as well as House Martins near the quay. Pied Wagtails were seen along the dyke & here I found a dead R.T.Diver. With Redshanks calling out & immature gulls in fair numbers flying overhead we departed from this scene after having good views of many species. [Written below: Avocet. S. Tern]
