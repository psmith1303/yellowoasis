Nature Notes 1959. Page 022

…resavoir, I paid a quick visit to Donyland Woods here I saw the “Windflower” out in abundance, but I never heard the Chiff Chaff which I purposely came out to hear. I left around 4.30 & the weather had turned quite cold.

[Written in pencil: “Yellow Wagtail”]
