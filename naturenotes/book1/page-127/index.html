9.11.58
Thorpe-Le-Soken

Invited by J.F. to go with him, & Mr Rose to see the Wildfowl collection of Derrick Lord. Russell Leavett came along as well. Down the road leading to Mr Lords farm we saw a Redwing flash by, very much like a Swallow. Then excellent views of a Kestrel on a telegraph post, & later when watching the wildfowl a Fieldfare “chacked” over.

Mr Lord’s collection is very nice & the birds condition were excellent, & plumage lovely. We saw first a mixture of ducks including - Red Crested Pochard, Common Pochard. Widgeon. Gargeney. Shelduck. Shoveller. Teal. Gadwell. Chilean Teal. & Lesser White front geese. Ross’s gose, & Snow Geese, & one very fine Bewick Swan pen. Two other very colourful ducks were the Mandarin, & Carolina both sexes, & juv;

On another pond we were shown Scaup duck. Mallard Pochard, & Tufted duck, & Shelduck ♂ & ♀.
