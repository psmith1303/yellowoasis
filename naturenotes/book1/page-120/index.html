…the rarish Water Ermine ♀ was taken by J.F. from the grass stems & by far the most numerous was funnily enough the Rosy-wave. About two Mathews Wainscots were taken as well as the C. Wainscot. A fair number came to the sugar & among them were Dogs tooth & Angle shades, Hart & Dart, & a few more of the common species. By the bridge from the road way we noticed the board of Bird photographs by C. Rose telling the public of the nesting species on the sand, this was erected by the St Osyth Bird watching Society Secretary J. Mead.
