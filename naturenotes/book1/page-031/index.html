21.4.57
Goldhanger
1.30pm

From sea wall saw three pairs of Shelducks feeding with three Redshanks. Found Ferral ducks egg, & more broken, & a broken up nest in water ditch alongside back sea wall. Plenty of Greenfinches & Skylarks about. In hedgerows found three Blackbirds next, first forsaken 1 broken egg, & two whole, hard sat. 2nd had 1 egg only which was cold. The third contained chick only & no eggs. Further along hedgerow was a Hedge Accentor’s nest containing three lovely black downy chicks, I watched, & identified male, & female.

In Friday Wood in the evening found a Little Owl’s pellet at old haunt of nesting site. (not a hole in the tree any more, - filled up). Had a look around wood finding nothing else, but delighted by hearing Green woodpecker, & glimpsing it, & also hearing my first Cuckoo this year. Another first year record for me today was seeing my first Swallows c.a. 5. Saw Lingards on sea-wall basking in sun.
