9.7.58
Lexden hill area. No. 197
Whilst working at the above address R. Leavett noticed a Nuthatch on a Sycamore tree, & later on a bird table, this is a “first” for him. The occupant Mrs Epsom, has had as many as six Robins feeding together, & also a G.S.Woodpecker feeds at the table in winter. Once she introduced a few goldfish to a garden pond only to be taken very quickly by a Heron.
24.8.58
A visit to the resavoir today did not reveal many birds in the poor August day of winds, cloud & sunny periods. Over Layer Breton causeway saw the usual birds such a s G.C.Grebe. Coot Swan, Common Tern, & a few ducks well out. The other causeway was the same with the inclusion of Wheatear’s on migration and Yellow Wagtails. Whilst here met Mr Pyman, & exchanged a few words, & watched the Wheatears.
