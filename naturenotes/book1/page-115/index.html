22.6.58
Eriswell Brec, Suffolk

A very poor wet day, went to the brecks on motor cycle with my wife & family, & C.W. & R.L. Arrived at about 11.30 after chain breaking in Bury St Ed’s in time for lunch under farm barn.

Spent about an hour over brecks & saw many Pheasants, & Partridge Skylarks. & Starlings. Managed to see Stone Curlews, & Woodlarks, & very good views of Wheatears ♀ & ♂ first time birds for R.L.

Visited the field of Bugloss, & noticed there wasn’t any Grey Carpet moths on the wing, nor put any up, inspected Flixweed but no larva.
