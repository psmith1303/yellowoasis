CONTD

….apparently deserted, very cold, watch will be kept. Starlings, & 2 Pied wagtails were seen, both near water. Blackbirds nest reported in Cauliflower stump with young. The Flora found was interesting & early, a good variety was seen, & in river bed was River spurge. (Slimey grey mass). Wild Garlic was found in on particular spot on river bank.

No success in finding owl pellets. A Wren’s nest was found completed for eggs (I’ve found Wren’s nests ready for over a month before eggs have been laid).

Seen by Mr Richardson was a water vole. Young rabbits seen by Mr Firmin.

5.30pm

Arrival home.
