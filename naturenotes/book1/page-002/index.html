1945
Colchester. ABerton. Mersea. Peldon
4/4/46
Got 10 Moorhens eggs off Bourne pond. They were hard to get, and the water come up to my knees, There was also a Coots nest with 5 eggs, one of which I broke in an attempt which was unsuccessful, to get them. Thrushes nest in Wood Robbed.
5/4/46
Rainy Day. 1 Blowpipe, 1 Suction Pipe.
6/4/46
3 Long Tailed Tits eggs, from a Hedge near Roman River when F [?] was with Gall who had two eggs. Also I had an attempt to get at those Coots eggs with no success, we also went to the Redshankfield at Strood, on Mersea side for Lapwings egg, no success. Coming home we heard a Croaking heron, this was at a small Peldon Rookery which had no eggs.
7/4/46
The Chaffinchs nest found on 28/3/46 Had 1 egg. 1 Thrush’s egg was found in a nest which was in a yew in our garden.
