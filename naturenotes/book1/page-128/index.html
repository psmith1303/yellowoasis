Grass feeding were Barrack geese, & Brents (6) with the very spectacular Red Breasted Geese (4) with Grey legs. Bean, & White fronted Geese.

One hybrid was a wild captured Mallard X Widgeon. He also had a Garganey X Tea which we could not see.

It was very interesting, & a valuable morning for future recognition.

16.11.58
Whilst cycling along Mersea road near the cemetery at middlewick end saw about a dozen Fieldfare’s, with Song & Missel Thrushes, & Starlings, all feeding on Hornbeam berries.
