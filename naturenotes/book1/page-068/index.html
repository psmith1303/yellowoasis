27.10.57
Manwood Area

With Wilf & David walked accross the fields near Roman River near Ball farm where we found an owl pellet in an old hollowed Elm tree. Unknown species. Down by the river which had been cleaned recently are a few thick blackthorn, & from these flew about six Moorhens. One I watched few accross the fields towards the hollowed Elm.

3.11.57 Mersea. West to East.

From 7am until 1pm my sister Cynthia, & I walked the coast line around the Island east towards from Sea-view avenue.

The weather was quite awful raining very hard, & very windy from S/W.

The first species I saw was a Green Woodpecker flying accross the avenue. Plenty of Blackbirds about. Behind the first row of Beach Huts in the flooded swamp, we had a good view of the Redshank.
