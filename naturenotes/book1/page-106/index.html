7.6.58
Mersea

…in grey-brown down still, & could hardly flap their wings, a noticeable patch formed a ring base on the chest. The young were excellently camouflaged, & ran a very fast pace.

Not many more species at all to be seen except immature Herring or LBB Gulls.

We found Spotted Medic in the pathway to the water, & Birdsfoot trefoil. No inspects on the wing were seen, although patchy sunshine, it was very windy.

At West Mersea I collected approx half doz larva of the Brown-tail moth, & one Magpie Moth larva.
