…further on We found a Jackdaws nest with 1 egg, and in an ajacent tree found 3 more blotched eggs of the Jackdaw, the blotched was broken by Gord when gordon he got it out of the box. Near the Moat at Berechurch park, an owls nest (Noctura Passerina) contains three egg, 1 had two, and Gord the other, I swapped one with the Gaul for, one [?] unblown Redshanks egg. A Jackdaws nest nearby was building, and on the Bounsted Road had two eggs.
20/4/46 :
21/4/46
Got 4 Little Owls eggs from Friday Wood. Visited Jackdaws on Bounstead Road. Went to Wivenhoe park with Gall found Jackdaws with 1 eggs. then found a Tawnys nest with 4 hard sat egg all were cracked except 1 which I took, and when blown was useless. then found a pigeons nest with two eggs, I had one.
