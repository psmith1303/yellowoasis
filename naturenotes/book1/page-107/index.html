2.6.58
R.Backed Shrike

Fridaywood Farm area

…the colouring glory, & it was really lovely. Doubtless it is breeding in the field where I first suspected I saw it.

We were pleased to see a pair of Whinchats near the river side on gorse, & briars, & Blackthorn. We found two Early purple orchis although there are a good few of the bud plants in the slopes.

A vandal unknown picked a bunch which I found dropped in the roadway nearby, & there were easily fifty heads.
