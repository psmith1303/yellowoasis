15.11.57
Ardleigh
Whilst working on roof of farm building saw a flock of approx: 45- to 50 Fieldfares which uttered their “chack chack” note whilst flying over. The weather was quite dull all day, & only slight westerly wind, first I saw, an individual bird flying low across the field, with & slight undulating flight, & the greyish blue rump was very conspicuous. A Kestrel flew over.
17.11.57
A visit to the Abberton Reservoir where I saw my first Red Crested Pochard, with many Tufted Ducks & Mallard, & Common Pochard. Saw a fair sized party of Dunlin & a Pied wagtail, as well as many Gulls. A Cormorant was fishing well out. & took off, & flew away. The weather was very dull, & misty & cold, hardly any wind.
22.11.57
In one of the farm buildings @ Ardleigh Park Farm a Barn Owl site has been found plus two hundred or more pellets, & one dead carcase (a young bird still with down on it). Most pellets examined contained whole head skeleton’s of shrews. Some with 2 or 3 skulls.
