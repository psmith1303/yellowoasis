14.6.58
Brightlingsea

…to rear on sea-purslane. There were a few small Meadow brown butterflies, & Walls’.

Weather conditions were overcast but reasonably warm. Wind S.W.

14.6.58

Went to Brightlingsea for the afternoon, & called in Arlesford to see a Mr Johnson whom has captured an Albino Starling Juvenlie. Quite a nice bird, all white, pink eyes, legs & flesh coloured bill. Mr Johnson caught this bird about 8.6.58 when it was with normal young Starlings, it fed very well on egg yolk boiled, & bread, & milk also worms On the the 13 & 14th the bird seemed less bright, but looked quite well, & eating when I saw it in the afternoon of the 14.6.58.

[Tucked in: Cutting from newspaper. “Rare bird found in Alresford field”]
