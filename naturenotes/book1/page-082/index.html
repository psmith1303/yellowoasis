Further along the remains of a Redshank were found [TARSUS 2”] legs wings, & sternum only I kept the legs. The remains of a Teal were also nearby, which seems to me the death rate among birds this cold snowy weather must be fairly great. We returned home about 4-15. P.S.The head, & bill of the Common [Signed here C. L. Woolsey] Gulls, & the Brent Goose were kept.
30.1.58
At our society ornothology class the duck CW. & I obtained was identified as a first year Pochard, & by Witherbys handbook. Later, during the evening J. Sparkes brought in a male adult full plumage Smew, the first I’ve ever seen, & a lovely clear specimen, which was shot at the Abberton Res. John also showed me a Redwing, & Bullfinch ♀, which he had prepared as a skin.
31.1.59
R. Leavett described a bird to me, which he said flew by him very quickly, but noticing the yellow on wings, & extremities of tail. It very much fits the Waxwing? All this week Rooks have been at their Rookery at King Coles kitchen.
