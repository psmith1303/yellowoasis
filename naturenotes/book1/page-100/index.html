18.5.58
'DAWN CHORUS' Manwood - Friday

The CNHS held the annual “Dawn Chorus” in the Manwood-Fridaywood area from 2am until 6am. Four adults, & seven young boys attended. The Nightingale was already singing on arrival at 1.50am, & the first species of songsters was the Skylark at 3.40, & from then on the chorus increased in volume. The species heard were Robin late at 4.5am, Chiffchaff 5.30 Wren 4.10. W.Warbler 4.6. Hedge Warbler not heard. Rooks at 2.25 Manwood hill rookery, & 2.7am. Tawny & Little in competition at 1.25. Dunnock at 4.6am. No Blackcap until 6.0. S.Thrush at 4.1. Blackbird 3.55 Jay 2.40. Mallard 3.8am in R.River. Moorhen 2.50. Cockerill 3.55. Cuckoo 4.00 Pheasant 3.11, & 4.2. Yellowhammer 4.1. Pigeon 4.9. Whitethroat 4.30 recorded. Great Tit 4.37. Reed Bunting 5.00. Mallard Duckling seen at 5.00. Heron, heard, & seen at 5.10. Turtle Dove 5.11. Barn Owl seen g good views quartering at 5.15. Green Woodpecker heard at 4.30. Swifts seen at 5.10. Starling’s at 5.5. Lapwings seen flying at 5.50, & Jackdaw Magpies, & Swans. Recordings were made of Nightingale, & Whitethroat &…
