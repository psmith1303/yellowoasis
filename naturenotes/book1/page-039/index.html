2/6/57
Orford, Suffolk.

Took Pat, Davey & Russell to Orford in Suffolk, arriving there about 11 o’clock.

Walked along sea wall, and saw plenty of B.H. Gulls. Then the Oystercatcher was seen on opposite bank. The Shelduck was seen feeding opposite as well, then we saw a few pairs flying around. Observed another settled Oystercatcher, & had good view. After lunching along sea wall we saw Yellow Wagtails near fresh-water, plenty of finches flying around. Further along sea wall we saw our first Terns of the day. Just before crossing over sea-wall we found a Moorhens nest of seven eggs in reeds of fresh water ditch.
