12.10.58
Colchester & St. Osyth
Saw two Long tailed tits fly across East Stockwell street, from off a Sycamore tree, into a largish garden.
5.10.58

On society outing to Leewick saw about three Wheatears on passage. One Cormoratnt was perching on sign post. A party of Sanderling were seen for some time. A small number of Ringed plover were also seen on the shingle, & one Turnstone with them. Two or three immature G.B.B. Gulls were seen as well as a large party of adult birds, with a few hundred Lapwings with B.H. Gulls. A noticeable movement of Meadow pipits. A Kestrel was seen from the bus when proceeding to St. Osyth.

During lunch a good few caterpillars were found, of the Fox moth.

I noticed quite a lot of young Yellow Horned poppies, as well as two in bloom. Rest Harrow was found in bloom, & more exciting…
