Brecklands

…well away in the slopes. On two occasions I could have easily have caught a Leveret which both times was only three feet away couching frightened for a few second & then bolting away. A Carrion Crow was seen captured in a trap. The remains of a Hare which tried jumping the wire fence were seen, one hind leg was caught fast & showed how the animal had tried to break free. We had attempts at the Stone Curlews call but no luck although I had perfect views of ♂, & ♀ Wheatears. Occasionally we had glimpses of the Woodlark, but not very clear. We left at 7.00pm. Botanic specimens identified later were -: Common Melilot, Kidney vetch, & Spring Beauty.
