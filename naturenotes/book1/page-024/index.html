31-3-57
4pm
Went to Rookery near Reservoir at Layer side counted 38 nests in three trees of the
5.30
main Rookery. Climbed up, & found nests with eggs, handled one full clutch of five. Looked at Pellet of corn-husks mostly, & encoated with saliva which had dried to give glazed effect.

Noticed a flock of c.a. 300 pigeons 4 flying swans.

Visited Rookery in “Sparrow-hawk Wood” very many nests @ Tollshunt Darcy.

Found Thrush’s nest nearing completion up to mud stage, in field nearby S. H. wood.
