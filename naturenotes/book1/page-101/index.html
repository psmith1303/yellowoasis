…Wren, & a small interview with the C.N.H.S. Chairman, Mr Peck, & The Secretary & the boys in attendance. A very clear recording of the Nightingale with at least 14 pre-notes to full song. A Fox was heard at approx.: 2.40, a vixen probably calling young. Of great interest was a B Trout seen in Roman River by one of the boys. Very pleasing to me was the orchids found near the river.
