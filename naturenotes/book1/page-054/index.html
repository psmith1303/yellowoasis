18.8.57
Peldon Marsh, & Saltings

…gait, indeed I wished they really were. Seeing the young birds concluded a nice day out.

Went for all day walk with Cince & Russell along sea wall. The weather was quite bright, & a little windy, turning to small rain showers.

As soon as setting on the fields a Snipe flew off, & then we saw plenty of sparrows, & turtle doves, Starlings heard, & caught a glimpse of Corn buntings. The Redshanks flew up & called slightly, as soon as we got over the sea wall. Next we saw a pair of Curlews’ uttering their lovely call. Hundreds of Gulls were on the mud flats as the tide was out. B.H. & Common gulls. Pairs of Terns were having a good feed continually diving then moving along again.
