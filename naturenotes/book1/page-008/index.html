Further along found a Jackdaws nest with 6 eggs I had three.
22/4/46
Went to the 1st Fleets at Mersea and, got no eggs, but coming back from there found a C. Crows with 3 eggs, I had one, then we found a magpies nest with 3 eggs I had one. I climbed to a Carrions nest building. Then we went over the Redshank field, & Gaul found a, R-Legged Partridges nest with 5 eggs, I had one, because Lawton one the toss for a bad marked species.
23/4/46
—–
24/4/46

In the morning I climbed the old woodpeckers nest in the garden, and got 5 Starlings eggs broken 1, damaged 1, three successfully. Visited Chaffinches nest of 28/4/46 and it had youngsters. Found Thrush’s nest in yew tree near dump heap with youngsters.
