…& two lots of Cormorant in flight. Two Curlews flew across, & gave the very plain call. During the morning from 6.a.m. J.S. & N.S. saw & had lovely views of a fourth record of a Bluethroat in summer plumage, also a Ring-ousel, the other birds seen this evening were seen as well but more plentiful. Today the C.N.H.S. held their annual N.H. Exhibition which was very successful, & had many visitors. Exhibits were of high grade & plentiful, & a very good all round effort.
