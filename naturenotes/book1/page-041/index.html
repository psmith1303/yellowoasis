8.6.57
Abberton Res.

Observed on arrival the amount of Swans seen c.a. 150+. C. Terns were active, & seen from Layer causeway end.

Five Heron’s were counted from other roadway all on same bank. Pochards, & Shelduck were on reed-bed side in the shelter of reeds. The first Great-Crested Grebe I’ve seen flying took off, & flew in direction of main resavoir, I think the flight is quite strong, & quick & typical of Divers. Coots, & Moorhens were there, & the Yellow wagtails, & Mallard ducks, & ducklings (6). An unhealthy looking B.H. Gull was standing on the Grass bank, & did not fly at close approach.
