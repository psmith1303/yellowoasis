19.4.58
Cornfield Hart wood, Herts.

Bishops Storford.

With members of the C.N.H.S. visited the Cornfield Hart wood where we saw countless but beautiful Oxslips mingled with “windflowers”. In certain parts of this wood were the Oxslip x Primrose hybrid, & in another part Oxslip x Cowslip hybrid, all were very lovely.

Also found coming up were numerous Early purple orhis, & clustered bell flower, &

Dogs Mercury was very common. A specimen of Herb-paris was found. Barren strawberry was quite common.

Not many birds seen, but heard & seen was the Chiffchaff, & a pheasant.

In flower was the Blackthorn which is quite earlier than our area.
