…specimens. From here we walked out of the wood to the river were three Ducks few off. Going back to our cycles we collected two white “bluebells”, & then saw a fox go crashing through the under growth. Next we went to my suspected Owl’s nest, & on near approach the bird left an adjacent tree, it was very white in appearance & I now consider this to be a Barn Owl. Lower in a field other birds were mobbing, an owl which turned out to be a Tawny, & we had quite good views. It was getting darker by now so we left the area for home, & on parting I noticed a few Noctules in the air over grazing cattle.
