18.5.57
Manwood area
6 1.50 HRS
With the C.N.H.S. to hear “Dawn Chorus”. On arrival heard Nightingale right away. Then further down, & by the river in a willow tree low down - was heard a Sedge warbler attempts to see it failed although a bright flashlight was played on it. The bird was unaffected by this, & when switched off continued singing. Returned to car for rest of party, & then heard the Cuckoo which continued all the time we were there, until 5.30. rooks were heard then from Manwood Rookery, & previously by the bridge was heard a Curlew flying. The moon was up, & it was quite clear, but cold.

When the rest of the party arrived we split up in two parties, & went off to the river, & woods respectively. I my sister, & Russell went to the woods with Messrs Firming, & Peck & Kent.
