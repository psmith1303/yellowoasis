5.7.58
Leewick

With Joe Firm, & Paul Betts spent the night on the saltings at Leewick arriving at approx. 8.30. Weather overcast, & occasional drizzle, very light.

After setting up tilley lamp on old drum on sea wall, had a look around for Ground Lackey caterpillars of which we found about a dozen in my possession. Not many moths disturbed as we walked around, but did disturb Redshanks, & a pair of Patridges with young, & as we were nearer the sands heard a Little tern. Disturbed Rabbits as well. Among the interesting plants were Rest-Harrow, Yellow Horned Poppy, & one plant of Henbane.
