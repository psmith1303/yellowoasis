19.1.58
Mersea

Went to Mersea this afternoon to the east first where I saw two Cormorants perch on a river marker. There were countless Dunlin, & many Redshank. flushed about three snipes. A pair of Shelduck flew up the creekway.

At the western end observed my first good look at the Brent Geese. first I saw eleven in flight, & then got to within 80 yds of six feeding geese at a point opposite the Bradwell site, five kept close but one was away from the other all the time I watched. Not far away were, & couple of Curlew. A Cormorant arrived, & settled close by. Several Turnstones were close with the Curlew. Early this morning about five Goldfinches, & 1 Greenfinch were seen in the K.G.V. yard feeding on “Burdock” seeds.
