These nests are quite inaccessible, so inspection cannot be carried out & I hope deter any interference of them establishing themselves, & increasing. To me the “cawing” of a Rookery is so pleasing, & so interesting to watch, certainly a hive of activity. These three pairs will stay at nests when people are passing normally by when I watched them from inside the churchyard the flew off, & circled around but would not alight again until I retired.
31-3-57

Three nests will observed, & lessened activity probably now with eggs.

Also observed in churchyard in apex of church roof the Jackdaw is nesting.
