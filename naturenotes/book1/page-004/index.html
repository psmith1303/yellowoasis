13/4/46
Went out for afternoon with Gall, and Lawton we went on to the strood, and at Black Barn Rookery went up to a Rooks nest near the road. At the Strood fork road there is a field on the left. from here we got 5 Carrion Crows egg I had 2. A Moorhens nest at a small pond at Peldon no eggs, then another nest a Thrush’s with youngsters then farther on a Blackbirds with youngsters. We visited a Jackdaws nest with no eggs.
