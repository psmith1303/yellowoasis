21.9.58
Brecklands

With J.F. & Family, & J.R. went to the brecklands to show J.R. the Stone Curlews, we also unsuccessfully attempted a recording of them. We were rewarded by seeing about a dozen, & having very good views. Also we saw the Woodlarks, & heard them. There were no Wheatears - all had migrated as we expected.

The weather was cloudy, & sunny with patches of cloud now, & then. At lunchtime we checked on the “Motherwort” which was still flourishing well.

There were one or two Hares running about, & plenty of pheasants & Partridges. The remains of a Wood Pigeon were found, & one complete egg.

Whilst on the home ward journey we saw a Heron chased by a Rook.
