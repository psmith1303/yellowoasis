…got 7 Magpies eggs, of which I had 4. Then I found a Jackdaws nest of 5 eggs in a slit in a tree, could not reach them. Previous to that I visited a Jackdaws nest with no eggs, In Berechurch park. Visited the Jackdaws nest near. L owls nest near the Alsations, the littles Owls and the Jackdaws had not eggs, but in a Willow found 4 Blue tits eggs in a wet nest. Took 4 eggs from the Jackdaws nest on Bounstead Road, and broke two Magpies in the act, after this we found no more nests.
