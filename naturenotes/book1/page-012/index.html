Year 1947

20/3/47
Lawton got the first eggs of the season.

3 Rooks eggs from Black Barn.

21/3/47
Got my nest this day no eggs. Thrush’s.
22/3/47
Got my first egg of the season. 1 Rooks egg from Black Barn. 3 from Wivenhoe Rookery. We got 7 altogether in the afternoon.
23/3/47
Got 5 Rooks eggs from Clinghoe Hill Rookery.
