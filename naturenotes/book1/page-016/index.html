Dedham, Aberton

…Dedham, and another pair at Kiddles Farm.

Also heard my first Nightingale of 1947 in the Garden.

26/4/47 :
27/4/47

Got my first to Jackdaws eggs from “Our” Little Island not badly marked.

From Aberton Manor woods got my first 2 Pigeons eggs of the season. Also saw my first Sand Martins of the season, there was a countless numbers.

4/5/47

Got 3 Pigeons eggs from the Private Wood near “Our Little Stream, Broke 1.

Roy Monk came round after I got home, and he brought me 1 Black headed Gulls egg, 1 Pheasants, 1 Moorhens, 1 Blackbirds, 2 House Sparrows egg, and 1 Linnets eggs.

8/5/47

Thursday Heard and saw my first Swift of the season, there were 40 or 50 of them above South Street.
