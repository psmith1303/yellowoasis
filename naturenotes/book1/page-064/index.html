…Wood Sandpipers. We supposedly saw two, or three Greenshanks & definitely four Spotted Redshanks as well as the Common Redshank. A first bird for me seen after the Spotted Redshanks was the Little Stint - about four of them, quite smaller than the Dunlin they were with. Herons were there, & we counted about eight. The water was very low but, I think just turning to start filling. The Ducks were mostly, Widgeon, Mallard, & Teal, & on the Layer Breton causeway we noted a few Tufted Duck - one had a white undertail marking, & we considered this to be immature. Roughly more than a dozen G. C. Grebes were swimming well out. We arrived home by ten. Quite a big movement of Meadow pipits everywhere around the road. Linnets were perching on the Roadway fencing, & quite dull.
