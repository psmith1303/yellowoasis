14/4/46
Found Blackbirds nest down garden with youngsters, & 1 egg. Building Hedge sparrow in garden. Pigeons nest in cemetery ready for eggs. Went to Reservoir, and found no eggs, but in a field near found a Carrions nest of 4 eggs, 1 had two, for Lawton, and Gaul were with me, and we were very disapointed. Also saw 2 more Swallows.

19/4/46
Went for a ride with Gordon H, and found a Chaffinchs nest being built at Bounstead. Also that day found Jays nest ready for eggs, also a Pigeons. When with gordon we went to the Resavoir watching Cormorants on the other bridge, there is a field ajacent to it with a Carrions nest, which we get three eggs out, Sat. but I blew them…
