23.2.58
Abberton Res.

With C.W. went to the resavoir. The weather was very cold, with a westerly strong wind.

From Layer causeway saw c.a. dozen G.C.Grebes apparently courting on both sides of the road. A few Pochard were resting in a lagoon. On the other causeway we first saw the Heron, & Tufted Ducks. 5 Widgeon were feeding on the grass with the Ducks, & there were countless number’s of Lapwings. Again there were many ducks concealed in the grass sidings. On the reed-side was a large flock of Pochard, & Tufted duck nothing else with them. Parties of Coot hurried away when we approached the wire-fence.

On the way saw a ♂ Bullfinch disappear in the hedgerow. We left the reservoir, & continued to Birch Lake were we saw about four Tufted Duck. Collected “Pussy” Willow and Hazel catkins.
