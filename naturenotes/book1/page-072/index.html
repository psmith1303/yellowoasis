…was preening itself on the mud flats. Were there a few waders. Turnstones & Redshanks, a part of Dunlin were further ahead. Watched a flock of birds way out Bradwell reach & seeing J.F. a little later on he thought they were the Brent Geese he had watched them too, a little early perhaps.
