1.9.57
Abberton Reservoir
0600

With Neal Syer, & Mr Snoxall [M. S. J. Snoxell?], drove around road of reservoir.

The weather was quite warm but later it rained, & the temperature went down a little. The wind was slight in a westerly direction.

The water in the resavoir was very low, & we saw some good birds.

Upon entering the roadway in we saw Common Sandpiper & then my “first” Greenshank. Lapwings, - Gulls Common, B.H. & G.B.B. were seen, & the former three common, as were Pied & Yellow Wagtails.
