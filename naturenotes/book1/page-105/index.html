7.6.58
Mersea. East, & West.

With Pat, Cynthia, & Davey Woolsey to east Mersea were the very first birds we saw were a pair of ringed plovers & two young. After observations, through our binoculars we saw where the young birds hid when we approached, - this was under broken causeway concrete.
