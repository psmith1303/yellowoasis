25.5.58
Sussex

Joe Firmin, & I proceeded to Waterloo London where we linked up with Bill Simpson on an excursion to Sussex collecting Butterflies, & generally Botanizing. The weather was very fine sunny, & warm, & the insects came out well. A list of species is as follows; Butterflies….Speckled Wood. Wall. Comma. Orange Tip. Small Heath. Brimstone. Grissled Skipper. Duke of Bergundy Frittillary. Pearl Bordered Fritt, & Marsh Frittillary. Green Hairstreak. Green veined white. Wood-white. Small, & larg white. Brimstone one male dwarf specimen. Orange tip male, & female dwarf. Orang tip eggs, & larva found also of Brimstone.

Moths Speckled Wood. Brown Silver lines. Purple barred? Silver ground carpet. Wood carpet, & Cinnabar.
