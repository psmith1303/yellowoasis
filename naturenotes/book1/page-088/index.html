…in Brook st Col; early Feb, & third week March, feeding on Pyrocantha, & Rowan, & Hawthorn. J. Firmin saw six at Rowhedge 1957 in February.
4.3.58
Today R. Leavett, & myself again saw the Waxwings in Cambrai Rd. this time there were four birds in two pairs. We noticed the birds continuously making dropping, & we examined the berries which were very juicy. The birds tried each berry before consuming it, & if it was dry they dropped it. the berries beneath the tree were practically all shrivelled, & dry.
