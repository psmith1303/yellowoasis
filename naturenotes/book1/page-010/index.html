3/5/46
—–
4/5/46
3 Redshanks egg from 2nd fleet. I found one nest of 4, & Gall a nest of 3. We also got Jays egg I found nest of 3, and he found nest of 2. And a Jackdaw over near sandpits. Found Skylarks nest of youngsters.
5/5/46
Set out with Marg, found one Magpies nest with 3 egg near Abberton. Farther on we found another one youngster & 5 eggs. Then we went on to the Heron wood were we met Gall and Lawton, got no eggs. On to the Sparrowhawk wood five Jays eggs I had 2, and a Magpies five eggs. Windy.
6/5/46
One Greenfinchs nest 4 eggs.
