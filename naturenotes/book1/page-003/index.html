1946
Mersea Fleets Wivenhoe Rookery

Went to the Fleets, which is at Mersea Strood, with Gall, and Lawton, We found one nest of Coots with 2 eggs Lawton had 1, I had the other, blown with success. Found a Lapwings nest which was built, but it had no eggs. Saw Hundreds of Ducks.

In the afternoon we went to Wivenhoe Park Rookery, and I got 12 Rooks egg, including to clutches, 1 freak clutch which had a blotched egg. Blown in success. Gall received his eggs.

10/4/46

No eggs, but received 1 suction pipe, 1 Blowpipe, and 2 drills. That evening I went with Gall to a Carrion Crows nest down Bounstead, it had 4 eggs in it, and I had two, Ron [?] had the other two. One Robins egg of cince, and one Blackbirds egg of Gall which had a youngster. Useless.
