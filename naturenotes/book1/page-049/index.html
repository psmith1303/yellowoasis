15.7.57
Abberton Reservoir

With Russell Leavett toured the Reservoir, and on arrival saw Swans, & G.C.Grebes, & Coots with young at nest. Not a great deal of variety but found a skeleton of B.H.Gull. On touring round to further bridge saw the most swifts I’ve ever seen together, & hundreds of House, & Sand Martins. Plenty of young Coots, & Grebes on Reed side. Yellow Wagtails were about frequenting the sides, & the Reed Bunting. The Heron was seen by the trees on far side towards Layer Breton. A Barn Owl flew accross the reservoir from left to right facing Abberton, quite a long way. & we watched until out of view.
