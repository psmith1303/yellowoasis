10.4.57
Colchester
5.30
Discovered rather unusual nest of Blackbird, & Songthrush in same corner of hedge, within seven to eight fee of each other. Both birds were sitting. Weather was cold, windy & showery. @ 40 Recreation Rd. Col.
13.4.57
Attempted ascension of trees to Rookery in St Martins church yard unsuccessful. There appears to be more than six birds for the three nest, but the foliage is now thick, & I can only count three nests. Two pairs of Jackdaws nesting in church.
14.4.57
Went to Pin-Mill, the weather was cool, & sunny. Saw c.a. eighty Shelduck all in pairs feeding about fifty fee from pathway. Dunlin, or Redshanks were among them, also saw a pair of G. B.-Backed Gulls. Had a good walk around the area, & inspected many holes in trees for Owls or jackdaws nest; no luck, but did find Blackbirds nest, of four eggs in black …
