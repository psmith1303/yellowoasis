18/4/47

Blackbirds nest of 14th has now got young.

In a Junk shop at Mersea I enquired the price of some good Ostrich eggs: they wanted 10/- each for them, but they were not worth it for they were blown with two holes. and, Emus Eggs are rarer, and they are only 5/- - 8/- each.

19/4/47

Went to second Fleets today we got no eggs, but saw a pair of Heron, and a pair of Snipes, and a single one, also we found (D Gall) half an egg of a Duck species and the wing of a female Mallard.
