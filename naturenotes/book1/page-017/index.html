8/5/47

Got 1 Carrion Crows egg from Spinney before one gets to the Resavoir, there was 5 young, and this 1 egg. Got 4 Jackdaw egg off the road of Peldon to Tolleshunt D’Arcy.

Also got 8 Jackdaws eggs of one tree just past Peldon Post office on the right hand side of the Road.

10/5/47

Purchased 3 Black Headed Gulls eggs 3D each unblown off D. Gall, also 2 Mallards egg at 3D each 1⁄3.

18/5/47

Got five Jays egg from Aberton Manor, found nest of 2 Pigeon’s young, and a nest of a Pigeon with 2 hard sat eggs.

20/5/47

Purchased 4 unblown B. H. Gulls eggs of Gall, 3D each, and a blown Redshanks, and a Gadwell 8D.

22/5/47 : Cedar Found Pigeons nest of 2 hard sat and 4 H.S.* Blackbird egg in Laurestinas.

*H.S Hard sat.
