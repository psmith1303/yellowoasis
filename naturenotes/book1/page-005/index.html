…female Sparrowhawk, and every time we touched it, it turned on its back in defence, after this we left it in belief it had inturnal trouble. At the Sparrowhawk wood, there is a wood in a field with a pond, opposite the S. H. Wood here we got 7 Moorhens egg, I had three. Nothing in the S. H. Wood, but on the other side Lawton got 2 tawnys eggs, Gaul got 1 Magpies egg, I got three, near the Rookery. Coming away from there we found a long tailed tits with youngsters, and eggs, which we left. Coming home saw many birds including 3 (Noctura Paserina’s) Little Owl. P.S. Found one egg in a Carrions nest at Bonners Barn, and also heard my first cuckoo off the season but I may be mistaken, and saw my first Swallow near (percy [?] Christmas Pub). Kings Head Wigborough.
