4/4/47

Good Friday. Got 29 Rooks eggs from Reservoir Rookery 29 blown and drained.

Also 1 Tawny Owls.

Thrush nest of 21st still 4 eggs.

7/4/47

Easter Monday. Got 2 Owls eggs from old nest at Aberton.

14/4/47

Found one Blackbirds nest near new Wood Shed in yew tree with four eggs and, another along garden hedge with more.

16/4/47

Found Thrush’s nest in Holly no eggs.

17/4/47

Went with Roly and Day Raners. First one Thrush’s nest with 4 eggs in holly by stream. Then another with 4 near Mens Lavatory.

Another with 4 eggs near mens mess Room.

We found others except a Missels Thrush nest with the mother sitting on what could have been eggs or young, I could not…
