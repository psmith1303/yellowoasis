8/6/47
Found a Greater Whitethroats’ nest containing 5 eggs, the nest was in a briar bush in Hythes [?] Yard.
19.6. :

The only known breeding place of the Petrel (PTERODROMA INCERTA) is the island of Inaccesable, in the Pacific Ocean.

1.12.57

The following pages are records of N. History, & my egg collecting has been stopped since the last day opposite. The intermediate time I kept no records, I did not do much in natural history, time of courtship, & marriage.

The records are continued now I am a Colchester N. H. society member & a proud co-opted committee man.
