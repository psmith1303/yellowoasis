20/4/47
Went to Bounstead with Roly, and we got 2 Bluish coloured C. Crows eggs we had one each. We visited the Little Owls next in the Berechurch Park, but got chased, and coming away from there we see six Fox furs being cured.
21/4/47
—–
22/4/47

Saw my first Swallow of 1947 just before one is near Aberton.

Also got 1 Missel Thrush’s egg which Dad found on a grave near church and I got it off the window ledge were dad put it.
