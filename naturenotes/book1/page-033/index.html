3.5.57
Abberton Res. District
0615
Took Russell, Paul & friend bird watching. On arrival at Resavoir saw Great. C. Grebes, Coots, & Moorhens. Seven Herons. Terns. Skylarks. Shelducks, & Drake. Rooks, Black headed Gulls. Yellow Wagtails. In woodside hedges found nests of Yellowhammer, Dunnock & Linnet, then saw Yellowhammer, & Linnet. Blackbirds, & Chaffinch. Coming home visited Tawny Owl’s nest, & saw bird which had one egg. The nest was down about thirty inches, & the bird seemed to be ejected when it came out. A Pigeons nest was near with two eggs. One cracked by bird suddenly leaving. Other birds seen during the day included Swallow, Swift, house Martin, Jackdaw, Sparrow’s and Starlings. Pied Wagtail, Rooks, & swans (flying), & Pheasant, Greenfinch.
