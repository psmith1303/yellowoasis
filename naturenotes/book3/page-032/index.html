Park farm area & Friday Wood 30.12.62

BF, GE & AKW

Snow was quite deep blowing a blizzard & cold. Through the wood down by the “wikkiup” & snow looking very nice. Down to the river first seeing a pheasant & then flushing 4 or 5 Mallard from the river, as soon as these took up we saw a Water rail scurry to the bank & dissappeared. Up by the old woodpecker tree in line with the Ramparts two Woodcock flew off. GE arrived & to the Wikkiup lit a fire then had breakfast.
