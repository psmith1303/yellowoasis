Sunday 12.6.60

With G.E. & Son to Loch Pityoulish Garten to see Ospreys. This was from the RSPB hide. The view wasn’t up to our expectations & we were disappointed. Both birds were present the ♂ was sitting on a tree stump & the ♀ was in the nest, on top of a dead pine Spp?

Leaving here we continued to a few outlying villages namely Tomintoul & Glen Livet & Nothybridge stopping on glorious moor & hillsides we we caught & Mr Snoxell ringed 6 immature Lapwings. We were very delighted to see & hear snipe drumming & it sounded so much like the very shaggy long coated sheep. Many Curlews & Oystercatchers were seen & heard.

The first of the Red Grouse I have seen were close by the road…
