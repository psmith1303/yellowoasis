22.4.62

GE. BF. ND. AKW.

Fingringhoe Wick

Saw my first ‘62 Swallows & heard first Cuckoo, also saw first Sedge Warbler & GE saw Whitethroat, quite a few Sand Martins about. Many Shelducks on river mud & one or two Curlews, heard Redshanks & saw a few pairs of Mallards. Abundance of Coltsfoot in full bloom. Weather was warm & sunny. Moorhens were ween on the pols of this disused sand works.

Excellent views of a ♂ & ♀ Yellowhammer. Crows & Magpies & a Green Woodpecker were also seen here as well as Partridges.
