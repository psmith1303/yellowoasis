26.4.64 GE. BF. AKW.

At last a lovely day, started off by slight mist breaking into lovely sunshine & warm.

A Barn Owl flew from the tree down at the bottom of the lane by the ford & made B.F.’s Kestrel cry out.

We let the ♀ Kestrel of B.F.s free & it flew away happy & strong, after we secured a few photographs.

Starlings are nesting by the pond. A Heron flew up from the dyke & we saw our first Yellow Wagtail by the old pond. Mallard were flying around & Shelducks & Teal. BF & GE checked out the hole in the starling roost - empty. I went & climbed the Carrions Crows nest in the swamp/willow stand & it had one egg. I located two Blue tit nests.
