…Charlottes Grove, in here we saw a Red Squirrel at the nest of a Blackbird & the bird was attacking the squirrel furiously, we climbed up to the nest in a thick dry covered oak tree & saw five dead nuthatch young obviously killed & partly eaten by the Squirrell which quickly made off we photographed the nest & dead young, from here (where Archangel was also in bloom) we checked on the Stock Dove which was okay. We turned two sheep which were on their backs. Green & G.S. Woodpeckers were heard in Donyland. The Willow Warbler’s nest by “our” car park has got young.

/10/63 GE. BF. AKW. (First valley) records.

We caught a Grass Snake in Charlottes grove & obtained some decent photographs as the weather was mild & sunny intervals. After which we let it go back into the wood. Down by Layer brook we photographed a Common Newt which we fished from the new pond, also in here were thousands of stickleback fish
