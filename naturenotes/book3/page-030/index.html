16.12.62

Park Farm Area & Friday Wood. AKW BF.

A very windy, but sunny day & not to cold.

On arrival about 8 Magpies flew into an oak tree at the corner of the wood & Jays were making quite a din. After a walk down to the river & back up along the swampy ground seeing many Pigeons & a Herod & Fieldfares & Redwings & Blackbirds, the field here had been spread with chalk. After our snack we went through the wood & saw ♂ & ♀ Bullfinches in the roadway, we carried & noted that the Col was clearing the underground & had cut down the Scots pine in which the G.S. Woodpecker nested in during the year. We cut accross from the ramparts to fridaywood flushing a Woodcock on our way down in the dip by the Cols fir plantation. Up to the concrete road in the wood & just past we saw about four Goldcrest’s close up & a Marsh tit. Turning back we followed the little stream…
