17th & 18th May ‘64

On the Sunday morning down the valley with BF. & GE. Checked the orchids again & were in fine bloom my final count was approx 150 in the one field. Took numerous photos also of the Man Orchid which BF. brought from Kent. We split up & GE went to photo the Moorhens on the pond. B.F. went to check out sites over Ratcliffes & Platts ground. also to note Shelducks nesting near pond. The Starlings also in oak by pond. I went over to Mangolds lower meadow & saw a Grizzled Skipper & Orange tips photographed Archangel & Bugle.

Checked the Marigolds & Globe flowers, both in bud & look fine.

Sun shining well & quite warm - & very nice everywhere. Meeting up with GE & BF again we saw a pair of Winchats by the Willow stand near the river, also fine Yellow Wagtails…
