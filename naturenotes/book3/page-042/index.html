Nature Notes 1960 -- page-042

…Nuthatch which flew with a partner to Friday wood. We carried on along to the bridge at Park farm & along the river & over into the Col’s wood. Nothing seen in here but many Rabbit tracks & fox. We left at about 12 o’clock, the weather then was warm & sunny & very spring like in spite of the snow. G.E. was away home in bed with suspected Flu’.

J. Heath left the Colchester Nat His museum.
