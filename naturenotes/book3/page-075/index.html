…into the Badge sett, disturbing Rooks & Magpies near the church.

I made a seat up the tree ready for future Badger photography. Whilst in here a Lesser Spotted woodpecker was seen, soon followed by the G.S. potted again. The Lesser was calling quite a bit with an alarm note. Blackbird were dashing about and a Dunnock also Bullfinches. We fitted a pole near the other sett by the big fir trees in readiness for photography. Whilst looking around near the old gate house we had a lovely view of a Fox as it walked accross the path noiselessly & dissappeared in the wood.

Once again we went down to the Charlottes grove & Col Mangles had had the big oak trees lopped around the lower branches, also a clearing to the Fox “pipes”, here we saw a tree well used & holed by woodpeckers. Back in the wood again we saw the G.S.Woodpecker to our delight. We left the wood & then went off home.
