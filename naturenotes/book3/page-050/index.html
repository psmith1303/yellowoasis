Nature Notes 1960 -- page-050

Previous page Next page

Some messy project going on at the river lower down where a main is going over not much progress as we can see after a month. We crossed here & on up to the fields near the road, here we saw a Green Woodpecker on the ground, it flew up onto a small Oak & then on further to Rock farm area & disappearing from us. Pheasant seen here as well.

Along the river to the Willow stand or Copse more Magpies were feeding on the ground & on thistle heads. By the pipes we were pleased to see the Kestrel again & watched it fly into the large Elm & there was another perching as the other also perched near it. The perching bird flew off & call Kek!Kek!Kek!Kek! & then perched again as the other flew off. We departed after this nice closing episode. AKW.

Self heal was still bloom & Yarrow & Buttercups. A hare was seen in the marsh ground.
