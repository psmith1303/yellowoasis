…near this which has been in position for about 6 weeks. No life at all worth noting on the river. We inspected the hide & everything okay. Onward towards the pond we stopped as we saw a G.S. Woodpecker ♀ There were many birds up near the ponds as there are large thickets of Blackthorn & Sloe, where we saw Redwings & Fieldfares & Blackbirds again & at least six Magpies together. Brian flushed a Little Owl - first we’ve seen for a long while, this flew into the thickets out of sight & followed nearby by 3 pheasants. 3 Teal were flying around, trying to alight on the pond, when 11 more flew up with 3 or 4 Mallard. 3 more Mallard remained on the pond & did not fly off at our close presence - probably decoy ducks. Magpies seemed everywhere & two more jays not to mention the Redwings & Fieldfares.
