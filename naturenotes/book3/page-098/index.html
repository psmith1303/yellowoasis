Nature Notes 1960 -- page-098

Previous page Next page

…Made my way to where the Tree Creepers were nesting & watched them coming & going. A few Orange tips & Large & small white butterflies here. I checked for nests in the fir stand, but of no avail. Soon after B.F. came along & he climbed a nearby tree & photographed the Tree creepers. Time pressed on & we carried on into Charlottes Grove to the first Jays nest & found the nest empty, the next however had the bird sitting & as I climbed she flew off. We took flash pictures of the four eggs & then on to “Mas” house & coffee with Mr Sims.

We had an enjoyable morning with sunshine at times & quite warm.

Graham Longman lent me his baby Grey Squirrel to photograph, but conditions were not quite right, but I had an enjoyable time with a lively youngster. Next week to Dorset.
