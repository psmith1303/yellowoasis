13/1/63 Park farm Area & Friday wood B.E.F. G.E & AKW

Weather very cold & freezing, river frozen over in most places. Found starving Mallard by pipes & fed it & left it snuggled in the long grass. By the copse by the river we found a C. Crow apparently frozen to death, its wings were outspread, in this position we took a photograph of it. Birds see were wren. Woodcock flying over marsh & 8 Coot together below Dock farm bridge area.
