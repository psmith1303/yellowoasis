The return road was through Clachan & Daviot onto the main road back to Aviemore & Coylumbridge. (I must remark on the all night activity & calling of the Oyster catchers.) this round trip landed us back in daylight at approx 11.30pm.

Tuesday

Collected larvae from Juniper & search for O.Catchers nests & collected larva of the Northern Eggar. This occupied the Morning, During the afternoon with Mr Snoxell we made our way round to Auchleane via Glen Feshy, not befor we had stopped at ? to watch & have very good views of ♂ & ♀ Wood Warbler. My very first views. At Auchleane which comprises only one house near where we stopped the van & climbed a little way up the foothills of . collecting N. Eggar larva & some very nice green coloured Tiger beetles. From the furtherest point we went up to we could see through our glasses & observe about four or five deer & one looked a very fine stag very light coloured.
