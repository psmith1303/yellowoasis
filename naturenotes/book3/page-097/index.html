24th /5/64 AKW. & BF

We arrived soon after 6am & straightaway went down to Rock farm bridge over & accross to the Willow/swamp with the weather a little dull but warm & promising sunshine. I checked around the Poplars & willows for woodpeckers & B.F. watched a pair of Meadow Pipits & located a nest of four eggs. duly photographed & adults on fence. Found Water Horsetail in dyke near nest. Had breakfast (with cows) on logs by old pond & Starlings still in oak nearby & in dead Willow near that. Lapwings doing acrobats over marsh & Mallards & Shelduck flying around.

Whilst B.F. was photographing I collected an assortment of larva from the hedge of Sloe, also saw Wall butterflys & Small meadow brown. Thousand of insect in the air & many Martin’s and Swallow after these. Saw a fine Whitethroat & Yellow Wagtail ♂.
