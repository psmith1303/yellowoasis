…when we approached. There were a great many birds, but we could not even guess their numbers, this roost continued all through this area of dense thicket all the way up to Platts ground, in fact up to the Starling roost. When we arrived here the noise was terrific & the birds were roosting thicker on the Blackthorn & other trees thicker than leaves & looking like fruit Bats in sillouette. When we were nearer to these they flew up & moved further along, it was possible to get 12 ft away from them & they were not at all very visible when the light was right on them. We took several photographs with flash & this did not scare them as much as any noise we made. The noise was even louder when we got them all round us & when we scared them hundreds flew up & created quite a wind which we could feel blow on us. We had a very interesting evening.
