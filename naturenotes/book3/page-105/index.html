Our nearness to the next agitated the adults & gave out a fast high pitched pe pe pe - this also was the soliciting call of the young only not so loud. We we able to record this note. GE removed a bough from the half dead Elm tree in which the nest was excavated & whilst doing this was mobbed by both birds & during the sawing operation two young, very surprisingly flew strongly to the nearby hedgerow, some approx 50-60 ft away.

My presence up the same tree & 10 ft from the nest did the same thing & agitated the ♀ when came alone to fee the young 2 photographs were taken of the young coming up to the hole.
