…where a ♂ & ♀ had several young, upon being disturbed the birds called out & it sounded very much like Go back! Others were noted with young under the pine trees & whilst watching these we saw on the ground a very nice pale coloured Squirrel. The weather at this stage was cloudy, with sunny & warm outbursts but in between this with wind & rain it was quite cold.

On a very nice delightful shimmering Birch covered slope we photographed & collected some lovely Heath spotted orchids & Globe flowers as well as Marsh Marigold, Chickweed, Wintergreen, curious lichen covered scrubby birch & Butterwort, with every other corner filled with Polypody fern.
