Sunday 29th /3/64 GE. B.F. AKW.

Down to Roman River valley, which is still slightly flooded. Breakfast in “Wikkiup” watching Starlings investigation nest hols & heard Tawny owl. Through the wood & down to the pipes where we were pleased to see three of the first for the year - Sand Martins these were probably finding things hard going as the weather was windy & bringing much cold, overcast & with rain threatening. Many gulls still present & 1 pr Shovellers & a few Mallard & a Coot. Heard G.S. Woodpecker Drumming. By the flooded dyke & ground below the Manor we counted approx 60 Common Snipe which kept flying up in small parties as we got nearer. We were further pleased to see our first ‘64 Chiffchaffs three in all along the dyke, which we managed to snap shot, also three more House Martins & about 4 more over the flooded fields.
