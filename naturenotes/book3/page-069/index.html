…spare room, this we did with the hide from the willow - which to our dismay had bene pulled down by four young boys, we nearly caught when they came along the river bank carrying the hides & poles, after a long chase they got away & we recovered all except the strings, this incident disturbs us all very much, for as the weather gets better the area will be infested with people & studying from hides will be useless, - people cannot regard other’s property without interference. We saw Rooks, Magpies, Jays & a Fieldfare as well as Robin, Great & Blue tit, Starling & Dunnock & Bullfinch & Chaffinch. The old bark on the willows down in the stand revealed a nice pupa, to be identified if it emerges. We photographed this in posisition. We heard a Green Woodpecker accross the swampy ground.
