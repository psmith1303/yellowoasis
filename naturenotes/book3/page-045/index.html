Easter Mon

Park far Area. & Marsh Area. 15.4.63 AKW

A warmish dull afternoon. Green Woodpecker ♀ seen pecking at large pine in Charlottes Grove. Wood Anemones well in bloom, & Lesser Celandine. Marsh tits copting [?]. Heron flew over & a flight of swans. Many Swallows & first Sand Martins of ‘63 by river from pipes & a very nice ♂ Yellow Wagtail. Mallards flew over & Lapwings. Climbed up to C. Crows nest & photographed the nest with four eggs. Primroses in bloom by Rock farm bridge.
