We found a Fulmar nesting quite in reach & after getting about a yard away the bird ejected an oily fluid & then flew of revealing an egg as large as a hen’s egg & white with nest stains. These last mentioned birds Fulmar, Razorbill & Puffin all seemed to cock their rears over the cliff edge & defacated well clear of the ledge.
