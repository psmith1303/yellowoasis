…the wood. Whilst having coffee & debating the owl a nice ? G.S. Woodpecker came & perched in the very top of the poplars’ thin boughs, the soon made off towards the pipes. A Green Woodpecker was calling in the wood & twice flew around about us. We inspected a pine tree near the ramparts glade & noted a fairly fresh woodpecker hole. We came through the top of the wood & out onto the roadway, noticing an Arum plant with the spathe coming up.

The remaining few minutes we spent in photographing a plant of Cowslip with B.F. kindly brought back from Bury St Edmunds Suffolk.
