15.4.62

GE. BF. ND. AKW.

Park farm area. Very cold & dull.

Kestrel ♂ left the nest site of ‘61.

Green Woodpecker active & G.Spotted. Found a dead Red Squirrel under a pile of twigs in Mangles wood.

Seen & heard a Chiffchaff.

The Wed following this date GE, BF & ND Heard the first ‘62 Cuckoo.
