Dorset Purbeck Hills & Cliffs 29.5.64

With GE & BF first to Kent where we met Miss McDermott & colleagues to photograph the Soldier, Man & Spider & Monkey orchids also the nice Twayblade. The Soldier orchid in the wild garden of Dr Rose. The Monkey orchid was giving its best show to date & over a 160 heads were covered & protected with wire netting from Rabbits. The Fly orchid was indeed a nice delicate flower, we obtained some nice photographs. This was a find sunny & pretty hectic start to our holiday & the first nights camp in Sussex was very pleasant. The following (Sunday) we drove as far as the New Forest & had a look around & saw Grey Squirrel, a Cole tit’s nests & bushes of Butchers Broom. Lesser Spearwort & a nice patch of Heath Spotted orchids. When we were well into the forest we got very wet in a heavy thunderstorm…
