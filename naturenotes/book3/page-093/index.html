Kent

Left at 6.45

10.5.64.GE. BF. AKW. arried at 8.45.

To Kent & meeting up with Mr. R Foord & on to the chalkland south Kent downs to photograph the very nice Spider Orchids of which a good few were in bloom & the weather excellent. Later we met up with Miss McDermott & her party & we all went on to Queendown Warren Dale where we were fortunate to see & photograph the equally lovely Lady orchid & Butcher’s Broom. & more Spider orchids. Other orchids up but not yet in flower were Man & Heath orchid & Broad Hellorborine or White orchid. Fly orchid also was about 3 inches up & other Lady orchids. The Chalk Milkwort were a beautiful blue. Late primroses & Cowslips were in bloom & Herb Robert & Wild Arum. We had a very enjoyable day. We were amazed at the amount of photographers about 14 in all in the area & all with flash guns.

Our time of return was about 4.30 but not arriving at home until 8.30.
