1.12.62

Hinton near Walberswick AKW, BF, GE, RL

Went to see the Houbara Bustard, viewed at about 300 yds in a field of young Mustard. It wasn’t very spectacular although we had quite good views. On to Walberswick were we saw Teal & Widgeon, Moorhens & gulls & when we were leaving we saw two Bearded tits which flew from some fierce rails, these were good views. Weather. Very Cold.

[Pen sketch of Woodcock head.]
