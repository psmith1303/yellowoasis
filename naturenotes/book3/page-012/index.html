Nature Notes 1960 -- page-012

…hard going interrupted by the sight of three Ravens. A few more larva of Northern Eggar were also collected & a few plants when descending the cliff face, from there we made our way back nearly non stop & without incident, until we reached our van in the Rothy Murchus forest at about 11 o’clock & we once again saw the Woodcock. Not many moths about at all. GE jnr walked all the way & did exceptionally well for his age, perhaps he is the only boy of 8 years to climb up this mountain.
