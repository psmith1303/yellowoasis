…were more pleased to see a Greenshank as it flew circling around the whole swamped area, our first sight record for the area.

Nothing was revealed in the ex.Kestrels nest or yet the Stock doves nest, so we made our way into Charlottes Grove and climbed an old drey which was old & empty. The Bluebells showed a few heads already out in bloom & the Wood Anemones were well out. We were attracted by harsh bird calls of Jay, Missel & Jackdaws. etc & we guessed correctly that an owl was responsible so we decided to look in the old poplar & whilst we were below it we were pleased to see it swoop up to the hole & dissapear sitting nearby having coffee, we decided to try a photo, so B.F. donned the climbing irons & positioned himself ajacent to the hole & I climbed up the poplar, & when I was only 2 or 3 ft up, the owl left the tree very quickly, even before B.F. really saw it, then it sailed away accross…
