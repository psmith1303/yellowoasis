…& a good coloured Linnet. B.F. was lucky enough to see & photograph a Peacock butterfly laying eggs on nettles, (in the afternoon next day - Whit Monday I saw the same for small Tortoiseshell at the Sheepen). We were well pleased with the whole morning, Swifts, Swallows & House martins, & White throats were seen often. In the afternoon I cam back to the area of Charlottes grove to photograph, Wild arum & count the orchids, also to picture the Small copper. Quite a few people by the ford.
