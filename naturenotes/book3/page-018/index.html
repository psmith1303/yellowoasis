Sunday 25.3.62

AKW. GE. BF. ND later.

Fridaywoods

On arrival about 7.15am in GE’s car we parked off the road into the wood & we heard a G.Spotted drumming past the stand of pines so GE & Son went off to try & record the drumming. Brian & I went off down into the wood & he went round in one direction whilst I went straight through to the far easternside to the pine corner. Quite a bit of drumming was heard & pecking but no sight so far. The sun was just rising & as yet was very cold & a sharp frost. I carried on through the pines seeing Squirrel & young rabbits, Jays, Blackbird & Blue & Great tit, Magpie pigeons & Crows.
