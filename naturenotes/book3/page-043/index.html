BF. GE. AKW.

April 7th 196 3Park farm Wood & Fridaywood

Weather windy, cold but bright sunshine. Wild arum coming up well & photographed. Chiffchaff heard & seen first time 1963. In Charlottes grove & Fridaywood. Green Woodpecker & G.S.W. see & drumming & active, & L.S.W. heard calling. Nuthatches calling & seen in Fridaywood. Blue tits Great, Cole & Marsh quite active nest site hunting. B.F. & G.E. had excellent views of Red squirrel in Charlottes Grove.

Bullfinches seen frequently & 7 Jays. 2 prs Magpies, Crows & Jackdaws & Rooks active & a pair of Mallards on River near Park farm. Pigeons still quite abundant. Treecreepers seen (3) nest prospecting. A nice xx Yellowhammer singing in scrubland land West of Park farm. Celandine in bloom & Dog mercury.
