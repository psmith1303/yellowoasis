3.2.63

Park Farm Area & Friday Wood. BF. AKW.

Weather cold & still deep snow everywhere although the Roman River had thawed quite a bit thus giving the birds a chance to get scraps & freshwater.

The birds around the refuse dump were numerous with Gulls. Starlings & blackbird. We laid food out for them. Fox tracks everywhere. Along the Ramparts we had very good views of a G.S. Woodpecker which flew further on, it looked very well. Over to Friday wood still many fox tracks & Weasel & many Rabbits as well. Jays & Magpies & Pigeons & Bullfinches here & only 11 Siskins this week. After we had breakfast we packed up & as B.F. stood up he saw a weasel foraging around low growing bramble we watched it for a full 20 minutes & had very good interesting views until it finally saw us & disappeared quickly.
