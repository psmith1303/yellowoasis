1.3.64 GE. BF. AKW.

On arrival at Charlottes Grove we parked GE’s car and waked down beside the wood & inspected the traps GE had put down, we were well pleased to find we had caught a very nice Wood mouse in good condition which B.F. kept at home the other trap was sprung but not closed properly & nothing was caught. We had a better look when we transferred it to the tin by the pipes. We cross here & walked on to the willow stand, lit a fire & had breakfast. We saw Mallard here, flying near the pond & when coming away two Goldfinches were perching in the hedge. Along the ramparts we saw more birds the first was the Greater Spotted Woodpecker, the Jays, Magpies Robin, Bullfinches & a Chaffinch & Robin.

We also heard the Green Woodpecker calling over by the Willow/swamp area. We again parked the car in St Michaels church yard & took our kit…
