Sunday 1st April ‘62

ND, GE, BE, AKW.

Fridaywood & Parkfarm.

5 Squirrels. Lesser Spotted ♀ actively pecking in same oak as previous week & again seen I Mangles wood by GE & BF. Also ♂ & ♀ Greater Spotted in Friday woods seen by AKW in semi-courtship flight. One GSW in Mangles. Nuthatches very loudly calling in Friday woods & seen good views in Mangles. Jays. Magpies. Wren. L.tailed tits. Great & Blue.
