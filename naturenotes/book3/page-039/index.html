…very cold & severe weather with snow wind & fog & exceptional frosts of up to 30° have had a disastrous effect on the wildlife in all areas. Reports of many dead & dying Redwings & Fieldfares particularly as well as Coots thrushes ducks Wagtails etc. This effect was noticed at leewick where the following list of Birds were found dead & in the case of 1 Curlew & 1 Herring Gull were found in a sleeping position in the lee of the sea.

G.B.B. Gull 1. Shelduck ♀ 1. Grey Plover 2. Redshanks 16. Ringed Plover 5. Curlew 2. B.H.Gull 2. Herring Gull 1. Eider 1. Guillemot (Bridled) 1. oiled. Great Crested Grebe 1. Dunlin 3. Linnet 1. Meadow Pipit 1. This list only represents those which were actually found & seen the actual toll of dead birds must be fearsome. A dead fox in good condition was also found. Most of these birds were just flesh & bone. We noticed many live cockles…
