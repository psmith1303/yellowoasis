Sunday 2.2.64 Roman River Valley. AKW. BF. GE.

The weather this day was quite clear with a slight breeze & turning very warm during the morning. B.F. & I established G.E. in the hide & went over to the fir stand & here only Magpies were seen. We had breakfast at the south end of fridaywood, with a Robin for company. Jays & Pigeons were seen here & the Green Woodpecker. The Green Woodpeckers were quite vocal today & their notes was more flutey. We went through Fridaywood up to the road, just before then I watched a G. Woodpecker on a telephone pole which was calling loudly & then was joined by another only to part company very quickly. We intended to look for the L.S.W. but no luck with these although we carried on right through the wood to the M. CE seeing on our way a Siskin, Missel & Long thrushes, Fieldfares, Blackbird, Blut tits & more Magpies. Coming back…
