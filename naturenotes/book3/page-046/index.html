Donyland 10.5.63 RL. GE. BF. AKW.

6-AM to Ball farm to watch Fox cubs only saw Vixen, weather cold & dull. Over to Donyland Woods met R.L. who showed us the Nuthatch nest, watched a while, carried on to the clearing of conifers. Tree Pipit here & Cuckoo calling & Nuthatch again. Left the others to watch L.S.W. nest-hole & whilst here a Red Squirrel came beside me & gave lovely views at 6 ft. Archangel out in bloom. A Kestrel flew over. & hovered a while, Crows & Jays & Rooks, Gulls & Swifts active above the trees. Excellent view of Nuthatch very close. Over in fir wood Missel Thrush on nest. Goldcrest & Marsh tits calling & Jays & Goldfinches. Breakfast by car & whilst eating watched ♂ & ♀ Blackcap.
