Ball farm (Wood near here)

14.4.63

Park farm Area AKW, GE, BF

Tawny owl still in residence. Chiffchaffs & Willow Warblers singing well. The first two Swallows for us of 1963 alongside Charlottes grove & later near above mentioned wood. Green Woodpecker seen & heard, also G.S.W ♂ seen & one heard drumming. Jays & Magpies about, nesting & courting. Wood Anemone’s in bloom, but generally things still slow in plant world. Failed to dig pupa of Privet hawk where we planted them as larva on Privet shrubs where old cottage was long ago by the road. Red legged Partridges flew up near above wood. Bullfinches bright & quite tame. ♂ Chaffinches & Yellowhammer seen. Carrion Crow flew from nest in Ball farm Wood - Kestrel also seen in this area - ♂?
