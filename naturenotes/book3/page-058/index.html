12.1.64 GE. BF. AKW. Roman River Valley

A follow up to our previous Wednesday evening to the Starling roost at about 7.30am, we approached the roost from the Abberton road & stationed ourselves by the hedgerow & watched the birds depart. We concluded that most of the birds leave the roost in a northerly direction & some from each wave turn off & go in an East & West direction & hardly any at all to the South. From the position we were at the could not make an estimation of numbers. We went into the roost area when all the birds had left & there was much more droppings than before, from this we think the roost has only been occupied these last few (6 weeks). As we figured we surely would have seen these large flocks before the first date B.F. & AKW saw them. The weather was very overcast…
