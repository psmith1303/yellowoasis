Brian & I moved up into the wood again & then heard more loud drumming which too us up to the old sandpit (now filled in) to our delight we first spotted a Greater Spotted ♀ & then a ♂ lesser spotted which drummed lightly to be answered by the beautiful ♂ on the next old oak tree in our full view in good sunlight, the ♂ flew off but we watched the ♀ for some 1⁄2 hour as GE came along & also had good views with us as the bird fed & chipped away in the upper branches of the oaks, working quite systematically, it worked its way into the wood more & more & then it flew off further & we lost it then. We decided to have our coffee, whilst having this we hear Nuthatches calling & then the lesser spotted ♀ came, perched near…
