Nature Notes 1960 -- page-082

Previous page Next page

We saw a fox crossing the hilly field probably came from a dead sheep carcass at the corner of the Manor grounds. Celandines were in bloom & the Wood Anemone was coming up well. We went over to Fridaywood & heard the Green Woodpecker calling out & saw 4 to 5 Fieldfare at Col. Mangles bridge & tree creeper. In Charlottes Grove e followed a Red Squirrell quite a way & then lost it. We saw Bullfinches south of Friday wood & noticed the catkins of Aspen looking well. From here we made our way back past Marigolds Park Farm & the large patches of gorse in bloom & back to the car.
