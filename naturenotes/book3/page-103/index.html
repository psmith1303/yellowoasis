Nature Notes 1960 -- page-103

Previous page Next page

…to a woodland past Corf Castle & saw tame peacocks in the road & in a nice beechwood we saw a Buzzard & a Red Squirrell. In the wood we found numerous Heartstongue ferns & Toothwort & a patch of Damsons.

The week ended in a very wet day & we can well say that a lot of the holiday was very enjoyable. P.S. One unidentified Green Metallic beetle.
