…& this altered our ideas of sleeping in the New Forest, so we pushed on to Dorset & Worth Matravers where we found a suitable camping site.

The first night we go soaked through in the raining deluge which lasted at least 3 hrs. during the early morning & the rest of the first day was very wet, although we ventured to a spot called Dancer’s hedge along the cliffs & here we saw Puffins & Guillemots. Numerous Herring Gulls & Jackdaws & Cormorants & shags. We spent most of the week along the cliffs from the Winspit - where we met a character named Billy Winspit & very interesting & knowledgeable on the local history & birds - to St Albans head to Chapmans pool. At this last mentioned place we met a father & son - Sid & Allan Sanders & one afternoon had a glorious trip, in good sunshine in the Sanders boat…
