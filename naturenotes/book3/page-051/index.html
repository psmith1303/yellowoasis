22nd /12/63 GE. BF. AKW. Roman River & Thorpe

A perusal down the valley first. Quite dull & frosty we inspected the site over Platts ground where a lot of feathers are on & around the tree. Plenty of Redwing & Fieldfares about. Also Rooks & Crows & Magpies & Jays. The Heron was sitting in the usual tree by Abberton Manor. A party of Long tailed tits was seen & a Green & G.S. Woodpecker. A wren was seen near the Willow stand. A snipe flew up from near the copse where we moved the hide near a sheep’s corpse. Bullfinches also were quite numerous with Goldfinches. After breakfast we departed for Derrick Lords Farm at Thorpe & had a look around at his small pond with a pair of Bewick Swans, pairs of Shoveller & Red Crested Pochard. Herring Gull, Teal, Gadwall & Widgeon & Pochard. A Tufted Duck. On nearby fields we saw 200+ Golden Plover & Redwings & Blackbirds with Lapwings.
