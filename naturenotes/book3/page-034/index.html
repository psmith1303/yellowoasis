Nature Notes 1960 -- page-034

…26 approx & Bullfinches & blue tits nearby. A Green Woodpecker was seen earlier by the Mangles bridge. We cut accross the wood towards the Ramparts seeing more Jays & many Wood Pigeons. Rooks & Magpies & Dunnocks Redwings & Fieldfares. The snow was about 6 inches deep but the conditions for photography were poor & we only tried one or two.

We also saw Starlings & a Linnet & Chaffinch.

Woodpecker chippings were seen on top of the snow in Friday woods, fresh workings.

Gt Tit seen S. Fridaywood
