Wed 8-1-64 : BF. GE. AKW.

At 7.30pm we decided to have a go at photographing roosts - we had the Starlings in mind. The evening was quite mild & slightly misty, which made the evening very dark. We had a look in a hole in the tree in Charlottes grove, nothing in it so to another on the south of the wood this time an old woodpecker excavation about 6 inches into an oak tree - in this hole we found a Great tit which came out when we moved the bird with a pencil, it then flew into a holly tree.

We then made our way down to Rock farm bridge & heard many Redwings & possibly Fieldfare roosting in the thicket nearby - these birds did not like the sounds of us & the light from our torch, they flew further on down the hedgerow.
