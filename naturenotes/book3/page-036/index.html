20.1.63

Park farm area & Friday Wood & Shooting ground

A heavy downfall of snow covered the ground to a good 6” & deeper drifts in many places up to 3ft. Birds seem in a bad state of want of food we distributed quite a bit here & there. Activity very low among birds seen were Robin, Thrush & Blackbird. Blue tit, Gold finch, Bullfinch & Chaffinch. Jays & Magpie. C.Crow. Rooks. Jackdaws Redwings & Fieldfares & Tree Creeper. About 13 Siskins were in the Alder thickets. Moorhens were seen along the river & about 8 Mallard flew over. Also seen were Dunnocks & Starlings Fox & Rabbit & Hare tracks were seen in the snow & a lot of evidence of crossing the river were seen by these tracks, also Squirell tracks in the woods. Many pigeons still about. A G.S. Woodpecker was seen along the Ramparts. In Fridaywood was quite a nice party of Long tailed tits.
