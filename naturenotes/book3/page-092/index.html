27.4.64 First of Swifts over town.

3.5.64 Valley. BF. GE. AKW.

Raining again, but checked on the woods over near Ball farm & a nest of Jackdaw with 5 eggs. No new woodpecker holes. Green Woodpeckers calling also cuckoo which we saw. Wild Arum well out in ‘bloom’. From here we checked on the fox earth near Ball farm & saw it was being used. Many small holes in trees occupied by Starlings.

Checked suitable position for photographing Carrion Crows & checked next tree to see 1 egg at least still in nest. Moorhens nest in pond still six eggs also Starlings nest nearby. We came back to Rock farm & photographed eggs & nest of Chaffinch. Counted nine heads of Green Veined orchids. On to Berechurch hall. Not much more excavating by the GSW in the chestnut tree. White Comfrey in bloom on old hall site & pit full of water nearby with many newts. Badgers seem active in setts by the road. Progress of plants quite rapid.
