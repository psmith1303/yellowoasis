Nature Notes 1960 -- page-079

…all over, the trees in the roost were washed quite clean. Jays showed up frequently in flights & Blue tits & Meadow pipits. G.E. took a photograph from his usual standpoint on Radcliffs ground, also B.F. took one from Rock farm Bridge. We came back via Radcliffes ground noticing primroses in bud, down the hill & over by Rock farm bridge, not before GE & BF had a bootful of water each. The weather was so bad we could do no good staying & we went back to the N.A.H.Q. at 36. West Stockwell street. G.E. & BF. dried off. This is the first time the valley has been flooded to this exent since we have been going down.
