Nature Notes 1960 -- page-086

Previous page Next page

…Squirrel Drey. More bullfinches & a two Song thrushes fighting. Found a Dunnocks nest being built, also a Moorhen & nest nearby. From the marshes by the Manor about 2 dozen Snipe flew up. One or two Mallard were seen in this area. We have counted many dead sheep in this area over the last 6 months & this Sunday 1 fresh dead & 3 bodies in the dyke. Starlings are still numerous. Yellowhammers were singing well in Mangles meadow. Arum grows fast, one with Spathes & Spadix already formed. Back to the car from this area & noted the Jackdaws are nesting near the bungalow. We went on to Berechurch hall grounds & looked at the Badger setts where in some holes much earth had been moved. We were pleased to find a start of a supposed? G.S. Wood pecker hole in a avenue of Chestnut (horse) trees. Not a great deal of bird activity except Bullfinch, Blackbird & thrush & Pigeons & Carrion Crows & Jackdaws.
