During the evening we made up some sugar & lit the tilley lamp & went collecting moths until approx 1.30 the species so far are unidentified, a systematic list of birds etc to follow.

Wednesday

The weather was clear & warm as we set off to climb the mountain of Ben McDhui which is the 2nd highest mountain in Gt Britain at 4296 feet. A trek of about 8 miles was accomplished by our 8yr old G. Evans with us, to reach even the foothills. Beetles which at first are covered by an old pine forest & later heather moors & bogs. The track was very rough & hard going. At a point near where a hut for climbers is built we turned off to make our first steep ascent, the going here was indeed very hard over rocks & high heather & half way up at the time of 1.30 we had lunch.
