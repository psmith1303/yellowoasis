A dull morning with rain to come & after breakfast we packed up & off towards Rothiemurchus forest. Near Inverness there is a tip & then the firth where we saw many Hooded Crows & Carrion & Rooks & Gulls. At the water there were two flocks of Canada Gees & Gadwell & Mallard.

We arrived at the Rothiemurchus Forest in teeming rain & decided to still have a look around. We first went by the river & disturbed a female Roe deer. Collected water in our can to take home & then off across the track to Lairig Ghru pass. The scenery was as usual very nice & we saw 1 tiger beetle.
