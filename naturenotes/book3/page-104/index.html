14th Sunday GE. BF. AKW.

We located a L.S. Woodpecker nest with young, on the previous Wed, night & prepared to study the birds all day on the Sunday. GE & AKW watched & B.F. went off in search of the Kestrel’s nest. He reported no success & also the other Jay’s nest in Charlottes Grove was empty when GE investigated.

The parent woodpeckers were feeding regularly between intervals varying between 10 & 20 minutes nearly alternatively ♂ & ♀ to the loud cries of the young which as we found later were in a fledged state & appeared at the entrance hole frequently, their red heads showing clearly. The ? we noticed had got a pinkish head & when G.E. got within 4 ft noticed that this was made up of five red streaks as I saw myself later on. Mostly caterpillars were the main food, as far as we could see & both adults…
