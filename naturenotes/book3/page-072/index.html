…around the swamp & round to the pond. where two more Mallard flew off & 7 partridges nearby. Five pheasants flew out of the swampy rush covered area. A Common Snipe flew off as well & G.E. saw a Green Sandpiper earlier near the pipes. We cut accross to the new pond not seeing any birds here & carried on accross the fields to the Manor area, along this dyke we heard a Moorhen & watched a Kestrel flying fast down the valley & back again pretty soon. Two Herons flew up from the Dyke & made off in different directions, one going along & settling near Rock farm bridge had quite a bright orange bill.
