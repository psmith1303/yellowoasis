Sunday 24.11.63GE. BF. AKW.

Early visit to Fridaywood first, where Siskins & Redpolls, & Goldfinches were feeding on Alder cones, a Red Squirrel was also seen here. Out over the fields a Kestrel was seen hovering. Many Redwings & Fieldfares about & a large flock of Pigeon flew over.

During breakfast South of Fridaywood near main ride we sat & watched another Red Squirrel, also to our great delight we detected a Nuthatch tapping furiously on an Alder hanging over the river. Bullfinches were also seen here. Jays as well.

Walking along near Col Mangles bridge we saw a Kestrel again & a Heron flew off. Over to the Fir stand & again another Red Squirrel, more Redwings & Bullfinches & Blue tits & Marsh tits. The Heron had perched on its favourite tree by the Manor. We have a hide…
