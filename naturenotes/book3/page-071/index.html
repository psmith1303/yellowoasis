Sunday 22 Feb AKW. GE. BF.

Once again a very dreary day & raining cold at first & mild later. We rambled through the Charlottes grove first & soon lit a fire in the “Wikiup” & had breakfast, after this through to the bottom of the wood, not much stirring, only the Bluebells showing through well & Dogs Mercury. Past the pipes & along the river to Rock farm bridge, seeing very few birds on the way, Dunnock Blackbird Missel thrush & Blue tits. Green Woodpeckers were vocal earlier on. Over the bridge & B.F. turned over a piece of old timber & revealed a hibernating Newt, which G.E. photographed. We covered it up again, we carrying on along the river the other side now & a BH Gull was perching on a post by the river, a pair of Mallard flew off the river at this point as we walked on to the back Willow/Swamp area.
