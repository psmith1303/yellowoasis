Sunday 19.1.63 AKW. GE. BF. Leewick

After a very promising clear sunny week of cold weather, Sunday had to be very damp, cold & foggy & this dispirited us considerably as we had hoped to erect a hide & photograph some wading bird at Leewick. On arrival we saw Redshanks & a Cormorant & the usual G.B.B.G. Herring & B/H gulls. Quite a few Turnstones & Oystercatchers & one or two Sanderlings, about six Grey Plovers & 2 Ringed Plover. Quite a few Brent Geese flying around & one party of nearly 200 app. One Widgeon was seen & heard & a small party of Teal flew over. We saw a raft of Rd Breasted Merganser & easily recognised we two ♂♂ A good number of Golden Eye number over 200. Not many Curlew about or Redshank.
