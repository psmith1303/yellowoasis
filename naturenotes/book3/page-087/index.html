Sunday 19th April GE. BF. AKW.

Yet another poor morning with mist & overcast sky. Blackbird on nest in bramble & blackthorn clump near hedge opposite pipes. BF & GE saw 2 swallow & later we all saw a Sand martin. Over by the willow/swamp area we heard & saw our first Cuckoo of ‘64. Rooks, Crows & Jays & Jackdaws were busy most of the morning seeing all these birds frequently. Over Radcliffes ground we saw a Willow Warbler & heard Chiffchaffs. An abundance of Primroses beautifying the glades. A few rabbits around over the whole area. Not much activity by the Badger sett, but the Spurge Laurel seems to grow well in the hedge nearby. The foxes seems to be using the ‘earth’ up near the Windmill again this year. We inspected the old tree in the Starling roost, but no luck here, although another Blackbird on a nest.
