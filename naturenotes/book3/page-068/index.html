Only one Warden on duty now Mrs Simms

Sunday 9th 1964 GE. BF. AKW

To Badger ‘sett’ first thing & here we saw much new excavations, in the large old sett, also at the three outside setts. New holes have appeared inside big sett.

From the sett’s down to Charlottes Grove & then on to the willow stand by the river. The weather was against photography as we had planned to stop in the wood. We had breakfast here in the willows & had a cheerful fire, but not many birds were seen. Mute Swans flying over & a very pleasing sight of a Green Sandpiper wading in the Ford, this flew up & calling out loudly as it circled around.

We came away from the willows via Rock farm Bridge & inspected the old buildings there for possible places for roosting bats, of which G.E. had seen earlier near here. We were invited into Rock farm house by the Woman tenant for coffee & we spent a pleasant half hour, she invited us to leave what we would like to in here…
