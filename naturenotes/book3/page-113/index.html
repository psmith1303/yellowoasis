…Shags nesting below us were quite wary. The green eyes & plumage showed up very well & the Yellow edge beaks. The Raven came around this cliff once or twice. The weather was cloudy & sunny in intervals & afforded us good opportunities.

Wheatears & Rock pipits were flying around & Common & Herring Gulls. The Orkney isles showed up well & clear. We made back to the forest of Battan & seeing Hooded Crows on the way & Kestrels ♂. A Herring Gull was seen flying off the road carrying a large mammal. Shelduck on loch Fleet.
